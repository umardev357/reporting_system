Quick Guide to Reporting System

Here's a quick guide on how to use this webApp to create a container for a month and jobs

  1. Creating a Monthly Report Folder:
    1. Navigate to the "Create Monthly Report Folder" page.
    2. Select the year and month for the folder.
    3. Click the "Create Folder" button.

  2. Creating Jobs for the Month:
    1. Navigate to the "Create Job" page.
    2. Fill in the details for the job, such as title, date, category, etc.
    3. The job is automatically saved in the Monthly Report Folder based on the date the job was carried out.
    4. Click "Submit Job" button

  3. Viewing Monthly Reports:
    1. Go to the "Monthly Report Folders" page.
    2. Click on the specific Monthly Report Folder you want to view.
    3. You should see details about the month and a list of jobs associated with that month.
